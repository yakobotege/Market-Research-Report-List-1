<p><strong>The Tunnel Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transverse Tunnel Ventilation</li><li>Semi-Transverse Tunnel Ventilation</li><li>Longitudinal Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tunnel ventilation systems are essential for maintaining air quality and safety in underground environments. Transverse tunnel ventilation utilizes fans to blow air across the tunnel's width, dispersing pollutants effectively. Semi-transverse ventilation combines elements of both transverse and longitudinal systems, offering flexibility in airflow management. Longitudinal ventilation relies on fans placed along the tunnel length to create a continuous airflow path, promoting smoke control during emergencies. Each type caters to specific tunnel designs and operational requirements, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>

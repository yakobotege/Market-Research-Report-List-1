<p><p>Air Cargo Unit Load Devices (ULDs) are essential for transporting cargo efficiently in aviation. They come in two primary types: containers and pallets. Containers are fully enclosed structures designed to secure and protect cargo, ideal for fragile or sensitive items. Pallets, on the other hand, are flat, platform-like structures that allow for easy loading and unloading of cargo, accommodating various shapes and sizes. Both types optimize space and ensure secure handling during transit, contributing to the overall efficiency of air freight operations.</p></p>

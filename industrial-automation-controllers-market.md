<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial automation controllers market is typically segmented into analog and digital controllers. Analog controllers manage continuous signals, enabling precise control of processes like temperature and pressure through varying voltage or current levels. In contrast, digital controllers utilize discrete signals, processing data in binary form to execute commands, making them ideal for complex systems and automation tasks. Each type suits different applications, with analog preferred for simpler, continuous processes and digital for more advanced, data-driven environments.</p></p>

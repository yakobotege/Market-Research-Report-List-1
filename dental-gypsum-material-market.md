<p><p>The dental gypsum material market comprises three primary types: Dental Plaster, Model Dental Stone, and Die Dental Stone. Dental Plaster, known for its ease of use, is primarily used for making models and impressions. Model Dental Stone offers higher strength and accuracy, making it suitable for creating dental casts and working models. Die Dental Stone is the most refined, designed for producing highly precise dental dies used in crown and bridge work, ensuring optimal fit and aesthetics in dental restorations.</p></p>

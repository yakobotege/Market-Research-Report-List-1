<p><p>The DSRC Electronic Toll Collection System operates through two primary market types: DSRC on-board units (OBUs) and DSRC roadside units (RSUs). OBUs are installed in vehicles, enabling seamless communication with toll systems, facilitating automatic toll payment without stopping. RSUs, positioned along roadways, receive signals from OBUs and process toll transactions. Together, these components enhance traffic flow, reduce congestion at toll booths, and improve overall efficiency in toll collection, contributing to smarter transportation infrastructure.</p></p>

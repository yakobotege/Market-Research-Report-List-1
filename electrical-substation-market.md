<p><p>The electrical substation market is primarily categorized into Air-Insulated Substations (AIS), Gas-Insulated Substations (GIS), and other types. AIS substations utilize air as an insulating medium, making them suitable for outdoor applications and simpler to construct. GIS substations, on the other hand, use gas insulation for a compact design, ideal for urban environments where space is limited. Other types may include mobile substations and modular solutions, offering flexibility and rapid deployment for varied applications and needs in power distribution.</p></p>

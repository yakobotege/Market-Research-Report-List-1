<p><p>The Artificial Urinary Sphincter (AUS) market consists of two main types: the AUS with a balloon reservoir and the AUS with a spring mechanism. The balloon reservoir model uses a fluid-filled balloon to create pressure and control urine flow, providing more stable adjustment. In contrast, the spring mechanism relies on a mechanical spring to generate tension, allowing for quicker adjustments during use. Both designs aim to restore bladder control for patients with urinary incontinence, enhancing their quality of life.</p></p>

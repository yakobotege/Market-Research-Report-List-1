<p><p>PET preform equipment refers to machinery used in the production of preforms, which are the preliminary shapes for manufacturing PET bottles. Additionally, the adoption of automation and smart manufacturing practices is reshaping operations, leading to improved productivity and lower operational costs. The increasing demand for flavored water, carbonated beverages, and other liquid products is further fueling the need for versatile and efficient PET preform equipment.

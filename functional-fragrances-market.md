<p><p>The functional fragrances market is divided into two primary types: natural and artificial fragrances. Natural fragrances are derived from botanical sources, such as essential oils, herbs, and floral extracts, appealing to consumers seeking eco-friendly and sustainable options. In contrast, artificial fragrances are synthetically produced in laboratories, offering consistent quality and a wider range of scent profiles, often at lower costs. Both categories serve various industries, including cosmetics, personal care, and home products, catering to diverse consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>

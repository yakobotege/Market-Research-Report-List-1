<p><p>Distributed PV generation encompasses two primary market types: rooftop and ground-mounted systems. Rooftop systems are installed on residential, commercial, or industrial buildings, optimizing energy production by using existing structures and reducing transmission losses. Ground-mounted systems, on the other hand, are set up on open land, allowing for larger installations and potentially more efficient layouts. Both types contribute to decentralized energy generation, promoting renewable energy adoption while empowering consumers to harness solar power for local use and grid support.</p></p>

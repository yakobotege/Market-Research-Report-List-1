<p><strong>The Hybrid Electrical Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hybrid electrical cables market is primarily categorized into single-mode and multimode cables. Single-mode cables feature a small core diameter, allowing only one light path, which results in higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances, making them ideal for long-range telecommunications. In contrast, multimode cables have a larger core, enabling multiple light paths, which is suitable for shorter distances and lower bandwidth applications, such as data centers and local area networks. Both types serve diverse connectivity needs in various industries.</p></p>

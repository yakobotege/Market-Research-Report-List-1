<p><p>The Telemetry Monitoring System market is divided into two main types: wireless and wired systems. Wireless telemetry utilizes radio frequencies, cellular networks, or Wi-Fi to transmit data, offering flexibility and ease of installation, making it suitable for remote or difficult-to-access locations. In contrast, wired telemetry systems rely on physical connections, providing high reliability and uninterrupted data transmission, often preferred in environments where stability is crucial. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, from healthcare to environmental monitoring.</p></p>

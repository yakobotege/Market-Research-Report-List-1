<p><p>The medical heart stents market comprises three main types: Bare-metal Stents (BMS), which are simple, stainless-steel devices providing structural support; Drug-eluting Stents (DES), which release medication to prevent artery re-blockage; and Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffolds (BVS), which are temporary supports that fully dissolve after fulfilling their purpose. Each type addresses specific patient needs and conditions, contributing to the overall strategy in treating coronary artery disease while minimizing risks associated with stent placement and restenosis.</p></p>

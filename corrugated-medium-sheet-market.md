<p><p>The corrugated medium sheet market is categorized into various flute types based on thickness and application. A Flute is the thickest, offering excellent cushioning for heavy items. B Flute is slightly thinner and provides a smooth surface, making it ideal for printed boxes. C Flute is versatile, balancing strength and space efficiency. D Flute is thinner, often used for lightweight packaging. E Flute is even thinner, suited for shelf-ready packaging, while F Flute is the thinnest, used for smaller, detailed products. Each flute type serves specific packaging needs in various industries.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>CW Radars</li><li>Pulsed Radars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Short Range Radar System market includes two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) Radars and Pulsed Radars. CW Radars emit a continuous signal, making them effective for measuring the speed of moving objects and detecting proximity. They are often used in automotive applications. Pulsed Radars, on the other hand, transmit intermittent pulses, allowing for precise range measurement and object detection over varying distances. This type is commonly utilized in security, industrial automation, and transportation systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>

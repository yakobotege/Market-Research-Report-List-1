<p><p>The ceramic pigments market is categorized into two primary types: simple compound type and solid solution-oxide type. Simple compounds are typically composed of single chemical entities, providing vibrant colors that are stable at high temperatures. In contrast, solid solution-oxide types involve mixtures of various metal oxides, creating a broader range of hues while enhancing durability and stability. These classifications cater to diverse applications in ceramics, coatings, and plastics, meeting industry demands for color vibrancy and product longevity.</p></p>

<p><p>The electric energy meters market is primarily categorized into single-phase and three-phase meters. Single-phase electric energy meters are commonly used in residential settings, measuring energy consumption for smaller loads, typically up to 230 volts. In contrast, three-phase electric energy meters are designed for commercial and industrial applications, handling higher loads and providing greater efficiency through balanced power distribution across three phases. Both types are essential for accurate energy measurement, facilitating billing and energy management for various customer segments.</p></p>

<p><p>Barrier films are advanced packaging materials that provide protection against moisture, gases, and contaminants. The market includes various types such as PA (polyamide), known for its strength; EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol), offering excellent gas barrier properties; OPA (oriented polyamide), which enhances flexibility; and BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene), valued for its clarity and low cost. PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) provides superior barrier performance, while CPP (cast polypropylene) is often used for its versatility. Other barrier materials include biodegradable and specialized coatings.</p></p>

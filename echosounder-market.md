<p><p>The echosounder market is categorized into low frequency, medium frequency, and high frequency based on the operational frequency ranges. Low frequency echosounders, typically below 10 kHz, penetrate deeper but lack resolution, making them ideal for deep-sea exploration. Medium frequency (10-50 kHz) offers a balance of depth and detail, suitable for fisheries and oceanographic studies. High frequency echosounders, above 50 kHz, provide high-resolution images for shallow waters, making them essential for detailed mapping and habitat assessment. Each type serves specific applications based on the desired depth and resolution.</p></p>

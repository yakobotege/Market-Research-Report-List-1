<p><strong>The Multiple Zone VAV System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Duct</li><li>Dual-Duct</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multiple Zone Variable Air Volume (VAV) System includes two primary market types: Single-Duct and Dual-Duct systems. Single-Duct systems consist of a single air supply duct distributing conditioned air to multiple zones, allowing individual temperature control through VAV boxes. In contrast, Dual-Duct systems feature separate ducts for hot and cold air, mixing them at the zone level for precise temperature regulation. Both systems enhance energy efficiency and comfort in varying building sizes and applications, adapting to the specific needs of occupants.</p></p>

<p><p>Agricultural harvesters are categorized mainly into walking type and suspension type markets. Walking type harvesters, or handheld models, are designed for smaller fields and are operated manually, allowing precision in tight spaces. In contrast, suspension type harvesters feature advanced designs with suspension systems, providing better stability and efficiency in larger fields. This type supports various attachments, enhancing versatility and speed during harvesting. Both types cater to different agricultural needs, ensuring optimal crop yield and reducing labor intensity.</p></p>

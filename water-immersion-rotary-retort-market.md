<p><p>The Water Immersion Rotary Retort market is segmented into two types: Full Water Immersion and Partial Water Immersion. Full Water Immersion involves complete submersion of product containers in heated water, ensuring uniform heat distribution for effective sterilization. This method is often used for delicate products. In contrast, Partial Water Immersion allows only a portion of the containers to be submerged, which can be more energy-efficient and suitable for larger products. Both methods cater to varying processing needs and product types in food preservation and sterilization.</p></p>

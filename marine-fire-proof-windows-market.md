<p><p>The marine fireproof windows market is categorized into two primary installation types: welded and bolted. Welded installation involves permanently securing the windows to the vessel structure, providing enhanced durability and a seamless appearance, thus improving fire resistance. In contrast, bolted installation allows for easier replacement and maintenance by using mechanical fasteners, making it more flexible for various ship designs. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and compliance with maritime fire regulations, catering to different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>Depyrogenation tunnels are specialized equipment used in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries to eliminate pyrogens from containers and surfaces. Heated air circulating depyrogenation tunnels utilize hot air circulation to achieve uniform temperature distribution, effectively removing endotoxins. Infrared radiant depyrogenation tunnels employ infrared radiation for rapid heating, ensuring quick and efficient depyrogenation by targeting specific surfaces without extensive heat exposure to the surrounding air. Both types are crucial for ensuring product safety and compliance in sterile manufacturing processes.</p></p>

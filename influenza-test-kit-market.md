<p><p>The influenza test kit market categorizes tests based on the type of sample used. Serum samples, derived from blood, help detect antibodies. Plasma samples, which are similar but contain clotting factors, also aid in virus detection. Cell supernatant samples, collected from cultured cells, can reveal viral presence. Tissue homogenate samples involve blending tissue to analyze for infection. Additionally, "others" encompasses diverse sample types, including nasal swabs and throat swabs, catering to different testing requirements in diagnosing influenza.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Plastic Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aspheric Lens</li><li>Spherical Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic lens market primarily consists of aspheric and spherical lenses. Aspheric lenses feature a non-spherical surface, designed to reduce optical aberrations, improve image quality, and enhance visual comfort. They are commonly used in high-performance eyewear and cameras. In contrast, spherical lenses have a uniform curvature that simplifies manufacturing but may introduce distortion at the edges. While spherical lenses are widely used in budget-friendly applications, aspheric lenses cater to premium sectors where optical precision is paramount.</p></p>

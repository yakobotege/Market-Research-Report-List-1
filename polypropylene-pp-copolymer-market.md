<p><strong>The Polypropylene (PP) Copolymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotactic Polypropylene</li><li>Atactic Polypropylene</li><li>Syndiotactic Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene copolymer market consists of three main types: isotactic polypropylene, atactic polypropylene, and syndiotactic polypropylene. Isotactic polypropylene features a high degree of crystallinity, offering strength and thermal resistance, making it ideal for packaging and automotive applications. Atactic polypropylene is amorphous and flexible, used primarily in adhesives and sealants. Syndiotactic polypropylene combines properties of both types, providing excellent impact resistance and clarity, suitable for containers and textile applications. Each type serves distinct market needs based on specific performance characteristics.</p></p>

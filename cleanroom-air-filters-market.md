<p><p>The Cleanroom Air Filters market primarily includes HEPA and ULPA filters, each designed to maintain stringent air quality standards in controlled environments. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters capture 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns, ensuring high air purity. ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters are even more efficient, trapping 99.999% of particles down to 0.12 microns. Both filter types are essential in sectors like pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and electronics, where contamination control is critical for product integrity and compliance.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Angiography Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scoring Balloon Catheters</li><li>Conventional Catheters</li><li>DEB Catheters</li><li>Cutting Balloon Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angiography catheters market comprises several specialized types used for different cardiovascular interventions. Scoring balloon catheters have tiny blades that create micro-tears in arterial plaque, enhancing dilatation. Conventional catheters are standard tools for delivering contrast agents during imaging. Drug-eluting balloon (DEB) catheters release medication to prevent restenosis after balloon angioplasty. Cutting balloon catheters feature micro-knife edges that help in precisely dilating hardened arterial lesions. Each type serves unique functions tailored to improve patient outcomes in vascular procedures.</p></p>

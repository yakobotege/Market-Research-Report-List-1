<p><p>High Flow Oxygen Therapy Devices are categorized into two main types based on their oxygen adjustment mechanisms: automatic and manual. Automatic Oxygen Adjustment devices utilize advanced sensors and algorithms to continuously monitor a patient's oxygen needs, adjusting flow rates accordingly for optimal therapy. In contrast, Manual Oxygen Adjustment devices require healthcare providers to manually set and adjust oxygen levels based on visual assessment or patient feedback, offering a more hands-on approach. Both types aim to deliver precise oxygen levels for effective respiratory support.</p></p>

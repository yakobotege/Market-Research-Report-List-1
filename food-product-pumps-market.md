<p><strong>The Food Product Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive-Displacement</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food product pumps market can be categorized into two main types: positive displacement pumps and centrifugal pumps. Positive displacement pumps deliver a fixed volume of product with each cycle, making them ideal for viscous liquids and ensuring precise flow rates. In contrast, centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids, making them suitable for low-viscosity products and continuous flow applications. Both types cater to various food processing needs, ensuring efficiency, hygiene, and reliability in transferring food products.</p></p>

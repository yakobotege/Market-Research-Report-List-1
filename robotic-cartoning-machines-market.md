<p><strong>The Robotic Cartoning Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>End Load Cartoners</li><li>Top Load Cartoners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic cartoning machines market is primarily categorized into two types: end load cartoners and top load cartoners. End load cartoners are designed to insert products horizontally into cartons, making them ideal for flexible packaging and varying shapes. In contrast, top load cartoners place products into cartons from above, allowing for quick and efficient handling of bulk items or stackable products. Both types enhance packaging efficiency, streamline operations, and meet diverse industry requirements, including food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>

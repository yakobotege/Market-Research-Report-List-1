<p><strong>The Hydronic Floor Heating Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Above-floor Systems</li><li>Below-floor Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydronic floor heating systems are categorized into two main types: above-floor and below-floor systems. Above-floor systems involve the installation of heating pipes or panels on the existing floor surface, making them less invasive and easier to retrofit. Below-floor systems, on the other hand, embed pipes within the subfloor, providing efficient and even heat distribution but requiring more extensive installation work. Both systems effectively utilize water heated by a boiler or heat pump to warm spaces, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency.</p></p>

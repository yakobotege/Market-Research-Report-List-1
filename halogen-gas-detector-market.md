<p><p>The Halogen Gas Detector market includes two primary types: electrochemical and infrared detectors. Electrochemical detectors utilize specific chemical reactions to detect halogen gases, providing high sensitivity and rapid response times. They are often used in confined spaces due to their effectiveness at low concentrations. Infrared detectors, on the other hand, function by measuring the absorption of infrared light by halogen gases, allowing for non-contact detection over a wider range. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and compliance in various industries.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Acrylate Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylate adhesives market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic acrylate adhesives are versatile and can be reprocessed upon heating, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility and easy removal. In contrast, thermosetting acrylate adhesives cure permanently through a chemical reaction, providing strong, durable bonds ideal for structural applications. Each type serves distinct industries, with thermoplastics often used in packaging and automotive, while thermosets are favored in construction and electronics for their lasting strength.</p></p>

<p><p>Irradiation technology involves using specific radiation sources to sterilize, preserve, or enhance products. The market is primarily segmented into two types: cobalt and electronic accelerators. Cobalt irradiation utilizes cobalt-60, a radioactive isotope, to emit gamma rays, which effectively eliminate microorganisms. Conversely, electronic accelerators generate high-energy electrons to achieve similar sterilization effects without radioactive materials. Both methods are widely used in medical, food, and industrial applications for enhancing safety and extending shelf life, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The semiconductor package market comprises several key types including Flip Chip, Embedded Die, Fan-in Wafer Level Packaging (Fi WLP), and Fan-out Wafer Level Packaging. Flip Chip technology allows for better performance by connecting chips directly to substrates. Embedded Die integrates chips within a substrate for compact designs. Fi WLP features a single chip encapsulated at the wafer level, whereas Fan-out WLP expands on this by allowing multiple chips, improving thermal performance and miniaturization. Other packaging types address specialized needs within the industry.</p></p>

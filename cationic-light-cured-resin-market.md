<p><p>The cationic light-cured resin market includes several key components: oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, and additives. Oligomers serve as the backbone, providing viscosity and mechanical properties, while monomers enhance cross-linking and final hardness. Photoinitiators are compounds that initiate the curing process upon exposure to UV light, essential for hardening the resin. Additives improve specific characteristics such as flexibility, color, or thermal stability. Together, these elements contribute to the effective performance of cationic light-cured resins across various applications.</p></p>

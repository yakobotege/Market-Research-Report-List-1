<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Chamber Vacuum Heat Treatment Furnace market is segmented into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal type furnaces feature a chamber laid out lengthwise, ideal for processing long workpieces and providing uniform heating. In contrast, vertical type furnaces have a chamber oriented upwards, which is advantageous for maximizing space and facilitating easy loading and unloading of materials. Both types are designed to operate in a vacuum environment, ensuring high-quality heat treatment results while minimizing oxidation and contamination.</p></p>

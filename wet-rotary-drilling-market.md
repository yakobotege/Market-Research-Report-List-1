<p><strong>The Wet Rotary Drilling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Water Supply</li><li>Central Water Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wet rotary drilling consists of techniques that utilize water to aid the drilling process. The Side Water Supply method involves injecting water through side ports on the drill bit, enhancing cooling and lubrication while removing cuttings. In contrast, Central Water Supply delivers water through the center of the drill string, allowing for more effective circulation and improved cooling efficiency. Both methods are essential in optimizing drilling performance and ensuring effective handling of formation materials while minimizing wear on equipment.</p></p>

<p><p>The adult spinal orthoses market is primarily categorized into dynamic and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to support movement, allowing for flexibility while providing stabilization to the spine. They are ideal for rehabilitation and functional mobility. In contrast, static orthotics focus on immobilization and support, offering rigid structures to prevent movement and promote healing in cases of injury or chronic conditions. Both types play crucial roles in managing spinal conditions and enhancing patient care.</p></p>

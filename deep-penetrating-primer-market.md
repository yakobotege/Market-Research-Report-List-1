<p><p>The deep penetrating primer market is typically divided into one-component and two-component types. One-component primers are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing, offering convenience and quicker application. They are ideal for DIY projects and smaller jobs. Two-component primers, however, consist of a base and a hardener that must be mixed before application. This type provides enhanced durability and adhesion, making it suitable for industrial and heavy-duty applications where performance is critical.</p></p>
